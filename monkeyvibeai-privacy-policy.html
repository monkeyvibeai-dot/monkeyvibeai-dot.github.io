MonkeyVibe AI - Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how MonkeyVibe AI (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when using our internal tools and automations to publish content to Pinterest and review performance.

What this app does

  • Posts Pins to our Pinterest account (for example, daily images or video links).
  • Optionally reads basic analytics for our own Pinterest content (impressions, saves, clicks, etc.).

Information we collect

  • Authorization data: Pinterest OAuth tokens that allow our tool to post Pins and/or read analytics for our account.
  • Content data: Media (images/links) that we choose to upload and post.
  • Analytics data (optional): Aggregated performance metrics for our own Pins and account.

How we use information

  • To authenticate with Pinterest and perform actions we request (posting Pins, retrieving analytics).
  • To measure performance of our own content and improve future posts.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties except as required to operate Pinterest API functionality and as required by law.

Data storage and security

  • Access tokens are stored securely and used only for the purpose described above.
  • We take reasonable steps to protect tokens and posted content from unauthorized access.

Retention

We keep authorization tokens only as long as necessary to operate the automation, or until access is revoked.

Your choices

  • You can revoke the app’s access at any time through your Pinterest account/app settings.
  • You can request deletion of stored tokens by contacting us.
